About Me

I am a PhD student at both Macquarie University (Sydney, Australia) and University of São Paulo (São Paulo, Brazil) under a cotutelle agreement. My PhD thesis focuses on devising techniques to (semi)automatically repair a system-model when it fails to satisfy its specifications. In this context, I am combining Model Checking and the Belief change approaches in order to automatically repair system-models that do not satisfy the required specifications. My research revolves around Formal Methods, Knowledge Representation and Logics .
